Welcoem to Vortex

Vortex is an innovative AI-powered song recommendation system, built to curate a personalized music experience. By chatting with Vortex, you can discover songs that match your mood, vibe, or favorite genre. The recommendations are pulled straight from Spotify, ensuring you get the best tracks available.

Vortex isn’t just about discovering music, it’s about feeling the connection between your emotions and the rhythm of life. Whether you need something upbeat to energize you, or a calm melody to relax, Vortex will guide you to the perfect playlist.
